About Me

Hello, my name is Mubbashirul and I am a Cybersecurity Researcher who has learned almost everything on my own and I have a special interest in the cybersecurity threats that are present in our daily lives. Despite the fact I do not have any degree or cybersecurity certifications, I developed solid base during my practice, constant research, and self-education.

In particular, my area of specialization is concerned with educating people how to avoid typical cyber threats such as phishing, malware, and data loss. It is my intention to educate the general public with the necessary security knowledge so that everyone can work towards making the cyber world a secure place and ensure the safety of our future cyber space.

I love to educate the community on such knowledge and assist them in preventing themselves from falling prey to the vices of cybercrime through consultancy, advice and training on how they can protect themselves in the current technological world.
